How to Prepare VitalLeaf Matcha: Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1:
Preparation
Gather your matcha bowl (or any bowl), bamboo whisk (chasen), bamboo scoop (chashaku), and VitalLeaf Matcha

Step 2:
Measurement
Using the bamboo scoop (chashaku), measure 1-2 teaspoons of VitalLeaf Matcha powder into your matcha bowl

Step 3:
Add Water
Heat water to about 175°F (80°C) and pour a small amount (about 2 ounces or 60 ml) over the matcha powder in the bowl

Step 4:
Whisk and Enjoy
Use the bamboo whisk (chasen) to briskly whisk the matcha in a zigzag motion until frothy. Once mixed, slowly add the remaining hot water (6-8 ounces or 180-240 ml) and continue whisking until smooth and creamy. Your VitalLeaf Matcha is now ready to enjoy!
